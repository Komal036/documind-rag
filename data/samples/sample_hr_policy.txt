SECTION 3: REMOTE WORK POLICY

3.1 Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period
are eligible to apply for remote work arrangements. Contractors and part-time
employees must obtain explicit written approval from their department head.

3.2 Remote Work Days
Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. The specific days
must be agreed upon with the direct manager at least one week in advance.
Core collaboration hours (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time) must be maintained.

3.3 Equipment and Security
The company provides: one laptop computer, one external monitor, and a standard
peripherals kit. Employees must use company-issued VPN for all work activities.
Personal devices may not be used to access company systems without IT approval.

3.4 Contractor Remote Work
Contractors must submit Form RW-01 at least 5 business days before the intended
remote work start date. The form requires manager and HR co-signatures. Approval
is at the sole discretion of the project manager and cannot be appealed.

SECTION 4: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT

4.1 Approved Expenses
Employees may claim reimbursement for: business travel, client entertainment
(up to $150 per person), professional development courses, and home office
equipment (up to $500 per calendar year with manager pre-approval).

4.2 Submission Deadlines
All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being
incurred. Late submissions will not be reimbursed without CFO approval.
